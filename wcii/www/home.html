West Coast International Institute

Why WCII

West Coast International Institute is a not-for-profit educational establishment offering innovative and higher education programmes in an excellent academic environment. WCII has been a centre of excellence since its establishment, offering Certificate, Professional Diploma and Advanced professional Diploma programmes accredited by (NAQAA) National Accreditation & Quality Assurance Authority in the Gambia. These programs are further accredited and approved by our international academic partners globally.

QUALITY EDUCATION

Focuses on professional excellence, quality education, and lifelong learning, utilizing experienced staff with global expertise to ensure strategic growth and reliable learning opportunities.

UNIQUE SERVICES

Prioritizes practical education, focusing on critical thinking, collaboration, and tech literacy, to prepare students for success in modern workplaces and meet 21st-century demands.

What next after you complete your program?

West Coast International Institute has partnered with the African Institute of Management (IAM) in Dakar through a memorandum of understanding (MOU) for a Bachelor's Degree completion program. Students who finish our 2-year Advanced Professional Diploma can directly enter the final year at IAM in Dakar.
